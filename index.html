About Us

T. E. A is not a mere place but it’s an institute of English which has been developed for 30 years to become a great place emitting thousands of prominent citizens not only for the country but also for the whole world . all the persons who were born from T.E.A are capable enough to face any challenging situation fulfilling the mission of T.E.A ‘’ Enabling the younger generation to see the world through English

T.E.A was established in1987 . It’s founder is Mrs. Damayanthi Batagoda who was the assistant principal of Magedara secondary school . T.E.A is a primary and secondary English institute which is the oldest English institute in Yakkalamulla area . Now it has automatically become one of the leading English institutions in Galle

Though it is a widely spread vast place today , it was commenced with only 5 students in Mayakaduwa with the monthly fee of Rs.5/= . The first group who sat for GCE O/L in 1989 was thirteen students and all the 13 students passed English though they were from Mayakaduwa K.V and out of those 13 students 8 students got credit passes for English . From then every year many more students of T.E.A leave it to show their best talents tho the country

Today T.E.A is a huge tree with tightly held roots and widely spread branches giving thousands of fruits to the country to get the best use of them as prominent citizens

Thakshila welfare society is the backbone of T.E.A who decides what to do , how to do , whom to do and why to do new programmes for the benefits of students and the parents directing the principal too towards the success . Establishig the shrine room , seminars to the grade 5 students for scholarship exam . the prize giving. The sil campaigns of parents the religious programs such as pirith chanting ,alms givings , kapruk poojas are some of the unforgettable pleasant places done by Thakshila welfare society .The unfortunate and untimely death of the secretary of thakshila welfare society ,Mr.Sudarshna Vijesingha was a great loss for us but we feel he is immortal as he was the pioneer of almost all .

In this electronic era computer is the instrument which should be in the hearts of upcoming students .The computer unit of T.E.A gave the computer literacy free of charge with certificates, and the parents were provided with spoken English classes at nights free .The leadership training programmes for the students are held annually (by Mr Janath Nandasiri) to face the future challenges bravely . The prefect board of T.E.A paves the way to the betterment to their great service .Through the morning assemblies on Saturdays T.E.A gets much efforts to improve the speaking , recitation , and role playing abilities without Anglophobia and and stage phobia
